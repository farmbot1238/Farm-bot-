About

Farm Bot is an intelligent agricultural robot fully designed and built in Jordan by the students Haitham Al-Maaytah and Abdulrahman Al-Maaytah. It is designed to assist farmers and improve farming processes in a modern and efficient way. It monitors crops, tracks their growth, and identifies plants ready for harvest. It also helps maintain plant health by detecting harmful plants and removing them automatically. Farm Bot is capable of operating independently and making accurate agricultural decisions based on surrounding data. It can also be controlled and monitored remotely, providing farmers with detailed reports on the condition of the land and crops, which facilitates planning and decision-making. It contributes to increasing productivity and reducing resource waste, operates continuously throughout the day with high efficiency, and represents an ideal integration of modern technology with traditional farming. Farm Bot is the perfect farming partner for achieving smarter and more efficient agriculture, saving time and effort, enhancing crop monitoring accuracy, making farming processes smoother and more manageable, and serving as a step toward the future of smart and sustainable agriculture.
